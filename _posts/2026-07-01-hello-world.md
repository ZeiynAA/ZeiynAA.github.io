This is the first post on the site — and a template for the next ones. From now on you
just write in Markdown and hit publish; the newest post automatically jumps to the top of
the list on the home page and the Writing page.

### What this section supports

Paragraphs, [links](/writing.html), `inline code`, lists, and quotes are all styled to
match the rest of the site:

- Short, scannable list items.
- Consistent spacing and a comfortable reading measure.
- The same monochrome palette in light and dark.

> A blockquote for the occasional line worth setting apart.

That's the whole pattern. Keep writing — the newest post goes at the top of the list.
